Manual Pseudopotentials and orbitals Examples

ABACUS currently supports two types of the norm-conserving pseudopotential (SG15 and Dojo) in four different formats (UPF, UPF2, VWR, and BLPS). And ABACUS employs a new strategy to construct high-quality numerical atomic orbital (NAO) basis sets, where the reference wavefunctions and its first derivatives are both taken into account as the fitting target. For details of the construction and testing of this basis, please refer to Peize Lin, Xinguo Ren, and Lixin He. Phys. Rev. B 103, 235131.

 

NOTE:
  • the atomic bases are optimized for particular pseudopotentials, which means that when a pseudopotential changes, the atomic bases must be regenerated.
  • although the basis sets presented on this page have been carefully tested, they are provided without warranty. Users should test them adequately before using them in their applications.

For SG15 pseudopotential:
  • SG15 optimized Norm-Conserving Vanderbilt (ONCV) type of multi-projector pseudopotentials for PBE GGA functional.
  • Standard DZP orbitals generated for SG15 v1.0 pseudopotential under suggested orbital cutoff radii (Rcut) and reference energy Ecut = 100 Ry.
  • All DZP orbitals generated for SG15 v1.0 pseudopotential for various Rcut and reference Ecut = 100 Ry.
  • Standard TZDP orbitals generated for SG15 v1.0 pseudopotential under suggested orbital cutoff radii (Rcut) and reference energy Ecut = 100 Ry.
  • All TZDP orbitals generated for SG15 v1.0 pseudopotential for various Rcut and reference Ecut = 100 Ry.

 

For Dojo pseudopotential:
  • Dojo type of multi-projector pseudopotentials for PBE GGA functional.
  • Standard DZP orbitals generated for Dojo pseudopotential under suggested orbital cutoff radii (Rcut) and reference energy Ecut = 100 Ry.
  • All DZP orbitals generated for Dojo pseudopotential for various Rcut and reference Ecut = 100 Ry.
  • Standard TZDP orbitals generated for Dojo pseudopotential under suggested orbital cutoff radii (Rcut) and reference energy Ecut = 100 Ry.
  • All TZDP orbitals generated for Dojo pseudopotential for various Rcut and reference Ecut = 100 Ry.
